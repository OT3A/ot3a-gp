Introduction

Transportation is a critical sector to any country as it acts as the vessel for all other activities including individuals, enterprises, governmental, and commercial services.

Problem Definition:

Key drainers of transportation systems are accidents caused by unsafe driving patterns and critical incidents which affect safety. For example, a traffic accident on a highway could result in a chain of accidents. This phenomenon has happened quite frequently over the past years. Irresponsible driving attitude resulted in severe accidents and sudden utilities incidents such as pipeline bursts, which could cause even more damage if not contained in a timely fashion.

Timely accident detection and taking immediate action with respect to emergency health care of victims by informing an emergency center such as a hospital or a police station about the accident on time plays a vital role in human safety and road traffic management.


Motivation

When accidents occur on the road, their detection is often delayed saving the situation and this results in many disasters, such as additional accidents or the death of some people, traffic disruption that disrupts. people's interests, and much more. And Utilizing AI methods and techniques can function computer vision, and optimization to detect, spot, and act on such incidents suggesting the best ways to handle them.

Objective

National adoption of intelligent transportation systems is the current trend. these systems can use information gathered from cameras, sensors, and official information systems to operate more quickly and precisely. Any kind of accident detected is automatically sent as an alert to the required destination (ex: the nearest traffic units and inform the Civil Protection) to solve the problem as soon as possible and save people's lives and not disrupt the interests of others by creating alternative ways until solve the problem.


Benefits

Save people's lives

No traffic densities due to accidents

Finding an alternative to reporting road accidents
